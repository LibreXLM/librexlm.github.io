Yet with the growth of modern capitalism and the expansion of money supply, money has become increasingly abstract, detached, and in many ways dehumanized. Capital became both an impersonal tool of accumulation and a force of discipline. What once was a social relation has turned into a system where value is measured in purely mechanical terms.

Personal tokens have the potential to change this. They can reintroduce identity, community, and creativity into the monetary sphere.
